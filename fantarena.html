💬 what is fantarena?

fantarena is the community hub for all fanter players. chat with friends, find people to play with, share game recommendations, and flex your achievements.

👥 how to get started

👤 click your avatar to customize profile & upload pic
➕ click + next to friends to add people
💬 switch channels using the sidebar
👥 create groups - only host can add members
🎨 group hosts can set custom themes & settings
⚙️ customize theme & profile in settings

no restrictions! speak freely, have fun. mods keep things chill.
