💬 what is fantarena?

fantarena is the community hub for all fanter players. chat with friends, find people to play with, share game recommendations, and flex your achievements.

👥 how to get started

👤 click your avatar to customize profile
➕ click + next to friends to add people
💬 switch channels using the sidebar
👥 create groups for your squad
😊 use reactions, gifs, and formatting
⚙️ customize theme & sounds in settings

🛡️ chat rules

be respectful. no slurs, racism, or inappropriate content. mods keep the chat clean. have fun!
